Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions

  • How to contact with you?

    You can join our Discord server for the quickest support and inquiries. We also have a Telegram group you can join. Alternatively, you can email us at [email  protected] , but please note that we check email less frequently.

  • Why the client is crashing?

    Client crashes may often occur due to the Vulkan renderer. If experiencing such crashes, it's recommended to switch to the more mature and widely supported OpenGL rendering technology.

  • Unable to check KRX Client status, how to fix it?

    There are a few possible fixes. First, make sure that you have an internet connection. Then you can try to change your DNS server , use a VPN or restart your router.

  • Is it safe to use?

    KRX Client is completely safe. However, the binary file is obfuscated for security reasons. That's why your anti-virus might flag it as malicious but it's just a false positive.

  • Is it open source?

    No, it's a closed source. Why? Because we want to keep it undetected, that way it's harder to make an antibot. We also want to have control over it. We might decide to disable access to the client someday.
